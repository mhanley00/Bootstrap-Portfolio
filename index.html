About Me

Madeline Hanley holds bachelors in East Asian Languages and Cultures with a concentration in Accelerated Chinese, and Fashion Design. She has lived in the U.S., Japan, France, Hong Kong, and mainland China. Madeline is an avid marathon runner, cyclist, and triathlete, training and competing in her spare time.
